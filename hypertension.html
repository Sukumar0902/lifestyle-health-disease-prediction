🩸 Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

💡 What is Hypertension?

Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a condition where the force of blood against artery walls remains elevated. If untreated, it can lead to heart disease, stroke, and kidney failure.

⚠️ Causes of Hypertension

  • 🍟 Unhealthy diet (excess salt, processed foods)
  • 🚶 Lack of physical activity
  • ⚖️ Obesity or being overweight
  • 🍺 Excessive alcohol and tobacco use
  • 🧬 Genetics and family history
  • 😴 Stress and lack of sleep
  • 💉 Chronic conditions (diabetes, kidney disease)

🚨 Symptoms of Hypertension

Hypertension is often called the "silent killer" as symptoms appear only when complications arise. Possible symptoms include:

  • ⚡ Severe headaches
  • 🌫️ Dizziness or blurred vision
  • ❤️ Chest pain
  • 😤 Shortness of breath
  • 💧 Nosebleeds (in extreme cases)

🛡️ Prevention and Management

  • 🥗 Eat a balanced diet with less salt
  • 🏃 Exercise regularly (at least 30 min daily)
  • ⚖️ Maintain a healthy weight
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol
  • 🧘 Manage stress with relaxation techniques
  • 📊 Monitor blood pressure regularly
