Depression

What is Depression?

Depression is a mental health disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, and difficulty in daily functioning. It can affect anyone and may be caused by biological, psychological, or social factors.

Causes of Depression

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Traumatic life events
  • Chronic illness
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Substance abuse

Symptoms of Depression

  • Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • Loss of interest in hobbies
  • Changes in appetite and sleep patterns
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Feelings of guilt, hopelessness, or worthlessness

Prevention and Management

  • Engage in regular physical activity
  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Seek social support from friends and family
  • Practice mindfulness and relaxation techniques
  • Consult a mental health professional if needed
